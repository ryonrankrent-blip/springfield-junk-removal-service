Appliance Removal FAQ

How much does appliance removal cost in Springfield, MO?

Appliance removal pricing depends on the number and type of appliances, their weight, where they are located, and any disposal or recycling requirements. Request a free quote with a short description of the appliances you need removed.

Do you remove refrigerators and freezers with coolant?

Refrigerators and freezers are common items we remove. Units that contain coolant may need to be handled according to local disposal and recycling rules, so please mention the appliance type when you request a quote.

Do I need to disconnect the appliance before pickup?

It helps if appliances are already unplugged and disconnected from water or gas lines. If you are unsure, let us know in your quote request and we can discuss the details.

Can you haul appliances out of a basement or laundry room?

In many cases yes. Stairs, tight spaces, and the size of the appliance can affect timing and pricing, so please describe the location and access when you request a quote.
