Trash Removal FAQ

How is trash removal different from my weekly garbage pickup?

Curbside service only takes what fits in your cart on collection day. We handle the trash that's left over: overflowing bags, piles that built up, and bulky waste that the cart won't hold. We come when it works for you, not just on a set route day.

What kind of trash do you haul away in Springfield?

We remove bagged household garbage, piled-up trash, bulk waste, and the mixed junk that collects in garages, yards, and rentals. We do not haul hazardous materials such as chemicals, wet paint, or fuels. If you're unsure about an item, just ask.

How much does trash removal cost?

Pricing is based mostly on the volume of trash and how it's bagged or piled, plus disposal fees. A few extra bags cost far less than a full cleanout. Send a quick description or photos for a free quote.

Can you remove trash after an eviction or move-out?

Yes. Clearing leftover trash from rentals, evictions, and move-outs is common work for us. Let us know the size of the job and where the trash is located so we can plan the visit and quote it accurately.
