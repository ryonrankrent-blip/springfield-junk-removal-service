Heavy Clutter Cleanout FAQ

Will you treat us with respect during a heavy clutter cleanout?

Yes. These projects are personal, and we approach every one with patience and without judgment. We work at a pace you're comfortable with, check before removing anything you might want to keep, and treat you and your home with respect from start to finish.

Can you help find important items in the clutter?

Yes. Important documents, keepsakes, and valuables can end up mixed into the clutter, so we go slowly and set aside anything that looks meaningful for you to review. Let us know what to watch for and those items can be set aside during the process.

Do you provide biohazard or crime-scene cleaning?

No. We are a junk and clutter removal service. Biohazard, crime-scene, medical-waste, and hazardous-material remediation are not assumed and require separate confirmation. Requests do not include biohazard, crime-scene, or specialized sanitation cleaning. If a home needs that kind of work, it should be handled by a specialized cleaning company, with general clutter and junk removal as the focus of the request.

How much does a heavy clutter cleanout cost in Springfield, MO?

Every home is different, and the cost depends on how much needs to be removed, the types of items, and the access. Because these projects vary so much, the best path is a free, private quote after we understand the scope. Reach out and quote details are provided after the request is reviewed.
