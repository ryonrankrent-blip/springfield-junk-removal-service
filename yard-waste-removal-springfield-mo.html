Springfield Yard-Waste Rules to Know Before Cleanup

Yard waste may be restricted from ordinary landfill disposal. City Material Know How guidance says yardwaste, including leaves, grass clippings, limbs, and brush, is prohibited from disposal at the Springfield Sanitary Landfill and is accepted at the Yardwaste Recycling Center. Missouri DNR guidance also lists yard waste among materials that may not be disposed of in sanitary landfills.

Springfield offers separate yard-waste recycling and composting options through the city-managed Yardwaste Recycling Center. City guidance distinguishes materials such as leaves and grass from brush, oversized limbs, root balls, soil, rocks, lumber, and mixed trash. Facility rules, fees, hours, load requirements, size limits, and accepted materials can change. Customers who plan to self-haul should confirm current rules with the facility or official city resource before loading a vehicle.

What yard materials can be included in a quote request?

Quote requests can include common non-hazardous yard debris such as leaves, grass clippings, branches, brush, small limbs, garden debris, and storm-cleanup debris that is bagged or loose. Material type, size, quantity, contamination, and access are confirmed before scheduling.
