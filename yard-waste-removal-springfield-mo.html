Yard Waste Removal FAQ

How much does yard waste removal cost in Springfield, MO?

Yard waste removal pricing depends on the volume of debris, how heavy it is, and how easy it is to access from the truck. A few bags of leaves is priced differently than a large brush pile. Request a free quote with a description of the debris.

Do you remove large tree branches and brush piles?

Yes. Branches, limbs, and brush piles are common items we haul away. For very large logs or whole-tree removal, please describe the size and amount so we can plan the pickup.

Does the yard waste need to be bagged or bundled?

Bagging or bundling loose debris can help, but it is not always required. Let us know whether the yard waste is loose, bagged, or piled when you request a quote.

Do you offer cleanup after storms?

We can help haul away fallen branches and yard debris after a storm when scheduling allows. Reach out with the details and we will follow up about availability.
