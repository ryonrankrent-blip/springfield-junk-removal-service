Eviction Cleanout FAQ

What is an eviction cleanout?

An eviction cleanout is the removal of everything a former tenant left behind, including furniture, belongings, and trash, so the unit can be cleaned, repaired, and re-rented. Once you have legal possession of the property and clearance to remove the items, loading it all up and haul it away.

Do you handle the legal side of the eviction?

No. We are a junk removal service, not a legal or law-enforcement service. The eviction process and any required storage or notice of belongings should be handled according to Missouri law and your attorney's guidance. We come in to clear the property once you're cleared to have items removed.

How fast can you clear the unit?

We know turnover time matters, so eviction cleanout requests can be reviewed for faster scheduling. Same-day or next-day service may be available depending on the schedule and the size of the job. Call to learn what let you know the soonest opening.

How much does an eviction cleanout cost in Springfield, MO?

Cost depends on how much was left behind, the size of the unit, and the access. Some units are nearly empty while others are packed. Send a description or photos and your request will be reviewed with a free quote.
