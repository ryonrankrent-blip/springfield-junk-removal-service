House Cleanout FAQ

What does a house cleanout include?

A house cleanout means removing the unwanted furniture, appliances, boxes, and clutter from some or all of a home so the space is clear. We handle the lifting and loading room by room. You decide what stays and what goes, and we haul off the rest.

How much does a house cleanout cost in Springfield, MO?

Cost depends on how much is being removed, the types of items, and how accessible the home is. A single room is very different from a fully furnished house. The best way to get pricing is to request a free quote with a description or photos.

Can you do a cleanout for a rental or move-out?

Yes. We work with homeowners, renters, landlords, and property managers to clear out rentals between tenants and homes during a move. Let us know your timeline so we can plan around it.

Do I need to sort everything before you arrive?

No. It helps to set aside anything you want to keep, but we can work through the rest with you. If something is uncertain, we'll check before loading it. Share the details when you request a quote.
