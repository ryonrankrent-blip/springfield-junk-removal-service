Couch Removal FAQ

How much does couch removal cost in Springfield, MO?

Couch removal pricing depends on the size and number of pieces, whether it's a single loveseat or a large sectional, and where it's located. A quick description or photo lets us give you a clear free quote.

Can you take a sleeper sofa or large sectional?

Yes. Sleeper sofas, recliners, and multi-piece sectionals are some of the most common items we remove. They're heavy and awkward, which is exactly why pickup handles the lifting and loading for you.

Do you remove a couch from an upstairs apartment?

In most cases yes. Stairs, tight doorways, and elevators can affect timing, so it helps to mention the floor and access when you request a quote so pickup details can be confirmed during the quote process.

Will you remove just one couch?

Yes, single-item couch pickups are welcome, and other furniture or junk can also be included with other furniture or junk in the same visit. Tell us what you have and it can be included in quote details.
