How much does furniture removal cost in Springfield, MO?

Do you remove mattresses and box springs?

Yes. Mattresses and box springs are some of the most common items we remove. Let us know the sizes and how many so we can include them in your quote.

Can you carry furniture down from an upstairs apartment or basement?

In many cases yes. Stairs, tight doorways, and narrow hallways can affect timing and pricing, so please mention the location and access details when you request a quote.

Do you remove just one piece of furniture?

Yes, single-item pickups are welcome along with larger loads. Share what you need removed and we will follow up with quote details.
