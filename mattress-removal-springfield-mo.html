Mattress Removal FAQ

How much does mattress removal cost in Springfield, MO?

Pricing depends on how many mattresses and box springs you have, their sizes, and where they are located in the home. A single twin is priced differently than several king sets. Request a free quote with the count and sizes for the clearest pricing.

Do you take box springs and bed frames too?

Yes. Box springs, metal and wood bed frames, and headboards can all go along with the mattress. Just let us know what's included so we can plan the pickup.

Can you carry the mattress down from an upstairs bedroom or apartment?

In most cases yes. Our crew handles the lifting and stairs. Tight stairwells, elevators, and narrow doorways can affect timing, so please mention the location and access when you request a quote.

What happens to the old mattress?

We dispose of mattresses responsibly and aim to route usable or recyclable materials away from the landfill where local options allow. Recycling availability varies, so it isn't guaranteed for every mattress.
