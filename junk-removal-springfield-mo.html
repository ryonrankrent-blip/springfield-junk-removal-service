Junk Removal FAQ

How does junk removal pricing work in Springfield, MO?

Most junk removal is priced by how much room your items take up in the load, along with the type of material and disposal requirements. A few bags of junk are priced very differently than a full truckload. Send a short description for a free quote.

Do I have to be home for the junk pickup?

Not always. If the items are in an accessible spot like a driveway, garage, or curb, arrangements can sometimes be made without you being present. Mention this when you request a quote so visit details can be confirmed during the quote process.

Will you take a single item or only large loads?

Both. Your request can be reviewed for single-item pickups such as one couch or appliance, as well as full-property cleanouts. Tell us what you have and we will include it in the quote.

Can I get same-day junk removal in Springfield?

Same-day or next-day pickup may be available depending on the schedule and the type of junk. Call early in the day for the best chance and we will let you know what is open.
