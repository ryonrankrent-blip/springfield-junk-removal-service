## 2026-07-23 LinkMiner finding

The five-local-competitor comparison and full leading-profile classification are in `linkminer-competitor-backlink-analysis-2026-07-23.md`. The core conclusion is to prioritize a few real local/editorial relationships over competitor link-volume replication. Most visible competitor volume is spam, press-release syndication, scraper indexes, generic directories, or ineligible operating-business profiles.

## Required Candidate Categories

Citations; local links; junk/waste/hauling directories; chambers/associations; suppliers/partners; sponsorships; community resources; local news/editorial; property management; real estate; moving; storage; construction/demolition; estate; donation/reuse; recycling/waste resources; legitimate resource pages.

## Reject Register

Record inaccessible competitor-only links, dead pages, spam, PBNs, paid-link schemes, lead-reseller platforms, syndication networks, mass guest-post farms, comment/forum spam, and exact-match-anchor campaigns. A competitor's use does not override rejection.

## Linkable Asset Scorecard

Score 0–5 each: search demand, local usefulness, internal-link value, backlink potential, commercial support, source quality, low duplication, maintenance burden (reverse), entity-safety, and reuse potential. Recommend the smallest asset scoring at least 35/50 with no critical risk.

## Measurement

Track referring domain, live URL, target page, link attribute, anchor type, indexation, acquisition date, cost, outreach approval, rankings/impressions/clicks before and after, calls/forms, and removal date. Correlation is not causation; do not claim a link caused lead growth without evidence.
