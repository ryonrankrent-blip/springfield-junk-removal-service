## Outreach and measurement gate

Before any message:

1. Verify the exact page and editor/owner.
2. Confirm the proposed Springfield resource fills a real gap.
3. Prepare a short, individualized draft with no link guarantee, incentive, payment, reciprocal link, or false relationship.
4. Present the destination, recipient, message, and reason for owner approval.
5. Send nothing until expressly approved.

For every later approved attempt, record contact date, recipient, target page, proposed destination, response, live link, link attribute, anchor type, cost (`$0` for this phase), and follow-up limit. Do not claim rankings or leads were caused by a backlink without evidence.
