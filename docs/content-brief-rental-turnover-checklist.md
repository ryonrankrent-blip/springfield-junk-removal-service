# Content Brief — Springfield Rental Turnover Cleanout Checklist

**Status:** Local draft implemented; not committed, pushed, deployed, indexed, or used for outreach.

## Goal

Create a useful, linkable checklist for Springfield landlords and property managers preparing an authorized rental turnover. The page must organize documentation, access, item classification, official disposal research, and quote preparation without deciding possession, abandonment, ownership, notice compliance, or any other legal issue.

## Claims deliberately avoided

- No legal advice or determination that property is abandoned
- No representation of entry rights, possession, notice compliance, or ownership
- No guaranteed acceptance, price, schedule, pickup, recycling, donation, or disposal outcome
- No crew, truck, staff, license, insurance, partnership, or service-history claims
- No hazardous-material, biohazard, asbestos, refrigerant-recovery, or data-destruction capability
- No permanent facility hours, fees, appointments, or acceptance rules
