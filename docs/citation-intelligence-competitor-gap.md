## Required Classifications

- **Approved-safe candidate:** factually usable by the current web property and separately owner-approved.
- **Requires approval:** research supports it, but account creation, submission, edit, payment, or public claim needs approval.
- **Renter-dependent:** cannot remain accurate through handoff without renter/operator facts.
- **Legitimate operating-business-only:** platform requires the actual service provider.
- **Paid:** any fee, membership, sponsorship, expedited review, or required upgrade.
- **Duplicate/conflict cleanup:** existing record needs evidence before editing or claiming.
- **Reject:** spam, misleading, lead-reseller, syndication, ineligible, or unsafe.
