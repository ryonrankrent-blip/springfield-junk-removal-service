## Lead-generation-only site policy notes

Several major platforms tolerate **service-area businesses** but still require:

- A **real business** behind the listing (not a marketing shell)
- **Accurate representation** of who performs the work
- **Phone and website** under business control
- No **misleading** address or ownership

Google is the strictest — explicitly excluding lead-generation agents. Even on more permissive platforms (Bing, Yelp, Nextdoor), listing a site without a verified operating entity creates **NAP inconsistency and suspension risk** once the operator changes.
