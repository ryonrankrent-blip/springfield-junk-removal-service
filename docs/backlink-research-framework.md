## Purpose

Provide a repeatable, guarded process to:

1. Identify top-ranking Springfield junk removal competitors
2. Audit their citations and backlink profiles
3. Find **easy, low-risk, repeatable** link opportunities
4. Reject spammy, paid, or high-risk tactics
5. Score each opportunity before any outreach

## Explicitly rejected tactics

- Purchased backlinks or guest posts
- Private blog networks
- Automated directory blasts
- Fake review or testimonial schemes
- Competitor brand bidding in citations
- NAP submissions with invented address
- Spammy blog comments / forum links
- Foreign-language link farms
- "Free SEO" submission services
